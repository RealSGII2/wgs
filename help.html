Installing a Theme

To install a theme, find a theme you like. If you want to see it, click Preview. If you like it, click Add. Then, allow the slide to be copied into your Drive so you have the theme available.

Adding the Theme To Your Slide

To add the theme to your slide, head to your Google Slide. Next, click the Theme button at the top. There, you will see an Import Theme button. Click it. In the modal that will show up, click the theme you just installed, and click on Select. Click the theme, and click on Import Theme. You will now have the theme in your slide.
